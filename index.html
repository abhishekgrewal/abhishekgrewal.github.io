Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Laboratory for Social Machines at the MIT Media Lab advised by Deb Roy. I love studying data and people. My research interests lie at the intersection of Causal Inference, Machine Learning, and Social Network Analysis. I am passionate about (i) drawing insights from data, (ii) designing interventions, and (iii) running randomized experiments. I spent the summer of 2019 at Facebook's Core Data Science team studying users who bring people together.

Prior to coming to MIT, I interned at Yahoo! Labs in Barcelona and the Amazon Machine Learning team in Berlin. I spent one year in Paris and one year in Barcelona to do a M.Sc. in Data mining and Knowledge Management. I got my B.Sc. from Staffordshire University with a First Class honors in Computer Science.
