Bio

I am a physics Ph.D. student in the Nanoscale Science Department at the Max Planck Institute for Solid State Research advised by Klaus Kern, and Klaus Kuhnke. My research interests lie at the intersection of nanooptics, scanning probe microscopy, and exciton-plasmon dynamics. I try to understand dynamics of luminescence processes at fundamental scale of a single molecule. My skill set can be summed up as a mechanic with know-how of physics and data analysis with a hint of intuition.
